About Me

Hello! I'm Christopher Kumwenda, a passionate Full-Stack Web Developer based in Lusaka. With over 4 years of experience building digital products, I specialize in creating responsive, accessible, and user-centered web applications using modern technologies like React, Node.js, and TypeScript.

My journey into web development started when I built my first website for a local nonprofit. That experience ignited my passion for using technology to solve real-world problems and create meaningful user experiences. I thrive on turning complex challenges into elegant, intuitive solutions.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me contributing to open-source projects, writing technical blog posts, or exploring the outdoors. I'm currently seeking opportunities to join a collaborative team where I can grow my skills while delivering impactful products that users love.
